The best books 2021

Today I release with pride the best books I read in 2021. They are amazing, so I would everyone recommend to read them! The genres are very different, so I think there is something for everyone!

Let me see the books!

My Top 3 Books of 2021

1. The Diary of a Young Girl

also known as The Diary of Anne Frank, is a book of the writings from the Dutch-language diary kept by Anne Frank while she was in hiding for two years with her family during the Nazi occupation of the Netherlands

Anne calls her diary "Kitty", so almost all of the letters are written to Kitty.

2. To Kill a Mockingbird

The story, told by the six-year-old Jean Louise Finch, takes place during three years (1933–35) of the Great Depression in the fictional town of Maycomb. Nicknamed Scout, she lives with her older brother Jeremy, nicknamed Jem, and their widowed father Atticus, a middle-aged lawyer. They also have a Black cook, Calpurnia, who had been with the family for many years and helped Atticus raise the two children.

3. The Alchemist

The Alchemist (Portuguese: O Alquimista) is a novel by Brazilian author Paulo Coelho which was first published in 1988. Originally written in Portuguese, it became a widely translated international bestseller. An allegorical novel, The Alchemist follows a young Andalusian shepherd in his journey to the pyramids of Egypt, after having a recurring dream of finding a treasure there.
